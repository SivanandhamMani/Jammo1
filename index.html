🌙 Moon Timing — When Will the Event Occur?
If Moon touches the Udayam, the event will take place. The number of days is calculated from Moon's rays and its distance to Udayam.
📍 Formula
Days = Moon's Rays Distance (Rashi count to Udayam)
• Moon's base rays = 21
• Count the number of rashis from Moon to Udayam
• If Moon is outside Udayam → count anti-clockwise
• Multiply Moon's rays rashi count = days until event
Example — Tula Udayam, Moon in Mithuna
Moon is outside Udayam → count anti-clockwise

Moon's Rays21
Rashi count (Mithuna → Tula anti-clockwise)9
Days until event189
21 9 = 189 days from Prasnam date
🔭 Udayam & 10th House (Ucchiyalsindanai)
Wherever the 10th lord is placed, the question is related to that Bhava.

⚖️ From Udayam to Arudam — VEDA Rule
Used to say whether an event will take place or not.
Arudam in 6th from Udayam → Event will NOT occur Arudam in 8th → Event will NOT occur Arudam in 12th → Event will NOT occur
Even if the event takes place, the person will not enjoy the results.
Example — Mithuna Udayam
Mithuna Udayam + Snake in Udayam + Arudam in Capricorn.
Question: "Will I get a job?" → Answer: NO
Because Paambu is in Udayam, and Arudam (Capricorn) is 8th from Mithuna — this is a Veda position.
⚠️ Kavippu — Hidden Consequences
Kavippu shows the consequences that are bound to happen as a result of an event.
Example — Will I get married?
Kataka Udayam + Arudam in Makara + Kavippu in Vrischika.

Marriage will happen — because Arudam (Makara) is 7th from Udayam (Kataka).
But after marriage there will be no child birth — because Kavippu is in Vrischika, the 5th from Udayam (5th house = children).
Kavippu reveals the hidden consequences of the event, not just whether it happens.
🔴 Special Rule
If Kavippu is in Udayam itself → the questioner himself will have a bad time.

CHAPTER 1
📖 Introduction
🌟 What is Jammakol Prasnam?
There are around 36 major Prasnam Shastra methods. All vary with respect to calculation and fixation of Lagna.
One Jama = 1 hour 30 minutes. There are 8 Jamas in the morning and 8 in the night (8 outer Jama planets + 9 inner transit planets).
Like KP Horary, prediction is given at the time the astrologer calculates and the location of the astrologer — not the client's location. Client can live anywhere.
8 Essentials of Jammakol Prasnam
① Udayam — Present & future, approves all events
② Arudam — 5-min window, knows the question
③ Kavippu — Hidden things, loss, death, danger
④ Jammakol Time — The exact Jama at question time
⑤ Transit Planets — Inner 9 Gochara planets
⑥ Maandi — Death / Detrimental point
⑦ Knowing the Question — Planets in Udayam
⑧ 10th House — Ucchiyalsindanai (highest thought)
Validity of Prasnam question: 81 days. Stambhana planets → indicate religious conversion or inter-caste marriage in family lineage.

Inner planets = Root (9 Gochara transit planets)

Key Rule — Inner Always Overrides Outer

CHAPTER 2
🌅 Udayam — The Ascendant
🌅 Udayam — The Ascendant
Udayam indicates the present and future.
Udayam is powerful. For any event to take place it needs the approval of Udayam.
No planets in Udayam → Take Udayam Lagna lord. Indicates no urgency — just a casual query.
Udayam Lagna lord sees Udayam → Native will take all efforts to complete the matter.
Udayam lord retrograde → Efforts will not be successful.
⚖ Lord of Udayam — Eligibility
The Udayam lord's position from Udayam reveals whether the querent's question is valid and achievable:
6th or 10th house from Udayam → Positive signal for the matter
Weak or debilitated placement → Negative signal
Retrograde Udayam lord → efforts will not be successful
If no planets in Udayam, the Udayam lagna lord is taken — indicates the query is casual, no urgency
If the Udayam lagnam lord sees the Udayam, the native will take all efforts to complete the concerned thing
⚠ Debilitated Planets in Udayam

🌀 Arudam — The Question Window
Time duration of 5 minutes. Neglect the hour hand — consider only the minute hand to cast the Arudam Lagna.
It is powerful. Knows the intention of the question put by the querent.
Everything past is Arudam. News we hear is Arudam.
Arudam in 3rd or 11th from Udayam → Success Arudam in 6th, 8th or 12th → Failure

Udayam = Lagna → ruled by Sun

Subha Arudam: Houses Cancer → Aquarius belong to Moon. If Arudam falls in any of these rashis → Subha Arudam. Otherwise → Asubha Arudam.
Verdict

Both Udayam & Arudam Asubha → Bad / Unfavorable results
⚠ Apply only when there are no planets in Arudam and Udayam

💎 Diamond Rules of Jammakol Prasnam — Detailed
1. Jama Graha Approaching Udayam 2. Jama Graha Crossed Udayam 3. Placement of Arudam from Udayam 4. Placement of Kavippu from Udayam 5. Exalted & Debilitated Graha 6. Inner Gochara & Outer Jama Graha
💎 1st Diamond Rule — Jama Graha Approaching Udayam ▼
Whichever Jama Graha is approaching the Udayam, the question will be related to the karakatwas of that planet.
Case 1 — Sun Approaching Udayam (Simha Rashi)
Questions relate to karakatwas of Sun: Father, 1st son, Heart, Right eye, Government job, Status, Politics, Prestige, Promotion etc.
Case 2 — Mars Approaching Udayam (Karkata Rashi)
Questions relate to karakatwas of Mars: Land, Brother, Husband (for female), Teeth, Fights, Surgery etc.
Similarly — Karakatwas of Other Planets Approaching Udayam:
♃ Jupiter
Children / child birth, money, gold, higher education, banking, Teacher
☿ Mercury
Marriage match making, love affairs, education, agreements, publishing, astrology
♀ Venus
Wife (for male), money etc.
♄ Saturn
Job, longevity, servants
🌙 Moon
Mother, mother-in-law, food items, water, extra marital affair, blood related disorders
🐍 Paambu
Pregnancy, foreign travel, black magic, devil, alcohol, witches, spirits, poison
💎 2nd Diamond Rule — Jama Graha Crossed Udayam ▼
When a Jama Graha has already crossed the Udayam (moved past it in anti-clockwise direction), it indicates the past problems faced by the native/querent.
Example — Mars-3 in Meena Udayam-20
Mars at 3 has just crossed Udayam at 20 (since Jama Graha move anti-clockwise). Therefore the native would have already faced problems related to Mars karakatwas — land, brother, husband (female), teeth, fights, surgery etc.
💎 3rd Diamond Rule — From Udayam Placement of Arudam ▼
Whichever house Arudam falls from Udayam → the question will be related to that bhava's significations.
1st House: Question related to native himself
2nd House: Income, family, marriage
3rd House: Brother, communication
4th House: Property, mother, water, vehicles
5th House: Children, love affairs, mantra siddhi, higher education
6th House: Disease, debts, court cases, servants
7th House: Marriage, spouse, business partnership, signing agreement, 2nd child birth
8th House: Longevity
9th House: Foreign travel, pilgrimage, father
10th House: Profession
11th House: Elder brother, profits
12th House: Expense (medical), overseas travel
💎 4th Diamond Rule — From Udayam Placement of Kavippu ▼
Whichever bhava Kavippu is placed from Udayam → significance of that bhava will suffer. Any planet placed inside Kavippu loses its karakatwas.
Examples
Kavippu in 7th from Udayam → person will not get married.
Saturn in Kavippu → concerned person will not get a job.

💎 5th Diamond Rule — Exalted & Debilitated Graha ▼
✅ Exalted Planets
Indicate prosperity of the karakatwas of the concerned planet.
⚠ Always check Kavippu — exaltation loses effect if planet is inside Kavippu.
❌ Debilitated Planets
The querent asks questions about things he is incapable of performing. He will keep talking like a chatterbox — all words, no action.
Example — Sun in Mesha (Exalted)
Sun is exalted in Mesha Rashi → all karakatwas of Sun such as Father, Son, Government job will be positive / affirmative.
Similarly, if Sun is debilitated (in Tula), the same karakatwas will give negative / unfavourable results.
⚠ Same logic applies for all other planets and their respective karakatwas. Always note the placement of Kavippu.
💎 6th Diamond Rule — Inner Gochara Graha & Outer Jama Graha ▼
Inner = Root — indicates real strength. Inner Gochara planets must be powerful for outer Jama planets to matter.
Outer = Branch — only considered powerful if the corresponding inner planet is powerful.
Key Rule
If inner Venus is debilitated and outer Venus is exalted → Venus is still considered debilitated. Inner always overrides outer.
Example — Mars in Kumbha Udayam
Whichever planet is next to Udayam, we must see the karakatwas and bhavas of that planet.
Consider Kumbha Udayam and Mars in Kumbha → Mars is approaching Kumbha → the question will be related to brother, land and will be affected.
📜 Jammakol Prediction — General Rules
At the time of question, if the karaka graha is debilitated, or is with Kavippu, or Kavippu is in the house of the related question → it will give a negative answer.
Example — Father's Survival
Udayam = Taurus, Kavippu = Capricorn, Sun = Libra (debilitated).
Sun is debilitated + Kavippu is in 9th house (indicates father) → Father will not survive.
If Arudam is in 3rd or 11th from Udayam → indicates Success.
⚠ Note the position of Kavippu.
If Kavippu is in 2nd or 11th from Udayam → there will be several obstacles in performing that particular act.
If Udayam lord is placed in 11th, or if the 11th lord is approaching Udayam → indicates Success.
⚠ Note the position of Kavippu.
Example — Kanya Udayam
Kanya Udayam and Moon in Kanya → Moon is the 11th lord approaching Udayam → therefore it indicates Success.
